# Showcase Video Assets Instructions

Please place your video files in this folder.

### Requirements:
1. **Filename matching**: Name them `app-1.mp4`, `app-2.mp4`, etc., matching the IDs in `index.html`.
2. **Format**: `.mp4` (H.264 video codec and AAC audio codec) is highly recommended for cross-browser compatibility.
3. **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 widescreen or modern app screenshot ratio (e.g., 4:3 or similar mobile ratio).
4. **Optimization**: Keep videos under 5-10MB each for fast loading. Mute the audio track to allow autoplay on all modern browsers (browsers block autoplay for videos with sound).
5. **Fallbacks**: If no video is present in this directory, the website will automatically display a beautiful animated gradient placeholder matching the app's accent theme.
